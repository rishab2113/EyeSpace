About Us


How many time has it been that you enter your room, leave your phone, car keys and wallet on a table and 30 minutes later you don’t know where these essentials are?

Homes | Office Premises | Factories | Airports

Mission


The mission of MemorySquare is to ‘Have an eye watch for you, when you aren’t watching’. We’re not here to simply connect people to objects, but people to people. At MemorySquare we envision a world where people feel secure, efficient and empowered.


How it works?


With just a M 2 Camera installed in every room of your house, our application tracks and keeps a record of your essentials. It helps you find them, when you cannot.

We can detect thefts, intrusions, unexpected activity
& alert in real-time.
